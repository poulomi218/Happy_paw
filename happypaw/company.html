About Us

MPM Overseas is a trusted modern trading company based in Singapore established in 2021.Infact we are one of the most successful and fastest growing trading companies across the globe known for ensuring the delivery of its best quality services. We put money into and obtain safe, reliable, and environmentally sound goods like recycled ferrous and non-ferrous metals, metallic scraps, ores, agri goods from our own trusted sources in countries across the globe like US, UK, Latin America, Canada, Australia, West Africa, Central Africa, South Africa and export them to some prominent smelters, refiners, metal makers, factories and other manufacturing units of asian countries like, India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, South Korea, Vietnam, etc. Our company’s periodic scrap trade comprises of ferrous, stainless steel , aluminium, copper, brass, lead and zinc grades along with some high quality agri products . Our company specializes in fast and efficient trades of ferrous material and of non-ferrous materials. Additionally , we also trade in semi-finished and finished steels as billets and rebars. We also have expertise in specific agri goods like rice, cashew nuts, soyabean, etc. We connect companies across the planet with our consumers in different continents which makes us stand out on our own as we believe in working smart and effectively hard. MPM Overseas is globally recognised by various global organisations in the field of scrap and agro commodity trading.?
